How It Works

The Synthetic Image Detector uses a CNN-based deep learning model trained on real and AI-generated human face images. It evaluates pixel-level inconsistencies, lighting patterns, and facial symmetry to differentiate real faces from synthetic ones generated by GANs and diffusion models.
